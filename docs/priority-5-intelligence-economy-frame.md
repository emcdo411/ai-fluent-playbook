## Recommended Section: Why AI Fluency Is an Organizational Asset

Para 1 - The organizational productivity gap:
Research on AI adoption consistently shows that firms with identical AI tool access
show dramatically different productivity outcomes. The variable that explains the gap
is the quality of the evaluation architecture - how teams decide when to use AI,
when not to, what counts as good AI-assisted output, and who owns the consequences.

Para 2 - What this playbook builds at the org level:
This playbook is not a skills training program. It is an EC Layer upgrade for your
product team. Teams that complete the 30-Day Sprint establish shared evaluation
criteria and governance scaffolding that function as competitive infrastructure.

Para 3 - The competitive moat argument:
The practitioners and teams who build strong AI evaluation architectures earliest
will hold a structural competitive advantage that compounds over time - not because
of the tools they use, but because of the judgment systems they have institutionalized.
This advantage is difficult to replicate once established. Start now.
