# Priority 4 - Expand Author Bio and Social Proof

Impact: Conversion infrastructure, enables purchase decisions from LinkedIn
Effort: Low - 1-2 paragraphs
Placement: Author page / About the Author section

---

## Recommended Bio Structure

Para 1 - Who Tim is:
Tim Dickey is a product professional with X years working in [domain/industry].
He has helped [type of teams] navigate [relevant challenges].

Para 2 - What The Great Inversion is:
The Great Inversion is his framework for understanding the structural shift underway
in product work - where AI handles more of the artifact production layer, and the
premium on human judgment, consequence ownership, and ambiguity navigation rises
proportionally. He writes about this at greatinvert.com and publishes on LinkedIn.

Para 3 - Call to action:
Follow Tim on LinkedIn at linkedin.com/in/YOUR_HANDLE and subscribe to
The Great Inversion newsletter at greatinvert.com for capability updates.

---

Note: The instinct is to understate credentials. Resist it. For someone buying a
playbook about professional competence, the author's demonstrated competence is part
of the product. You are not bragging - you are providing the information the reader
needs to trust what you have written.
