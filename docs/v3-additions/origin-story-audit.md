## Origin Story Audit: The Great Inversion

The Great Inversion thesis is Tim's original intellectual contribution. It is not a
widely cited empirical finding, a named academic construct, or an industry-consensus term.

Presenting your own framework as established fact invites a reader to Google it,
find nothing, and wonder why an established concept has no external references.
Labeling it as your original thesis invites them to evaluate the argument on its
own merits - which is where Tim should want the conversation.

Recommended language:
Current (implicit): The Great Inversion is the structural shift that is happening...
Recommended: I call this the Great Inversion - my term for the structural shift in
what product professionals are paid to produce as AI handles more of the artifact
layer. The evidence for this shift comes from [Brynjolfsson / productivity data].
The framework for thinking about it is mine.
