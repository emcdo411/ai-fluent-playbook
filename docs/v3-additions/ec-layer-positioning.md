# V3 Addition - EC Layer and Evaluation Architecture Moat

Source: EBT Framework v2.0, EC Layer construct, McDonald (March 2026)

---

## What the EC Layer Is

EBT v2.0 production function: Y = A times f(K, L, D, AI, EC)
where EC is Evaluation Criteria quality.

Applied to the playbook: What Tim has built is a toolkit for upgrading a team's AI
evaluation architecture - the criteria for when to delegate, when to retain judgment,
how to structure human-agent handoffs, what counts as good AI-assisted output.
Tim has written an EC Layer upgrade product and positioned it as a skills playbook.
These are not the same market.

---

## The Market Positioning Gap

| Current Framing | EC Layer Framing |
|----------------|-----------------|
| A playbook for AI-fluent product professionals | Infrastructure for teams who want to out-evaluate their competition |
| Individual skills development | Organizational evaluation architecture upgrade |
| Personal career resilience | Competitive moat building |
| Buyer: individual practitioner | Buyer: team lead, VP Product, L&D department |

Both framings are accurate. The EC Layer framing reaches a larger, higher-value buyer.
