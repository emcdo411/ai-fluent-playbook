## The Problem

The playbook tool landscape (Appendix B), capability framing in Chapter 2, and
watch out for warnings are calibrated to GPT-3.5/GPT-4 generation. Several have
shifted materially by March 2026.

V3 Escalation: The governance criteria themselves - HITL handoffs, agent role
definitions - will decay at the next generation boundary, not just Appendix B.
Capability shifts are discontinuous, not gradual.

---

## Recommended Language

--- About This Version ---

This playbook was calibrated to AI capabilities as of Q1 2026. The tool-specific
guidance in Appendix B and the agent capability framing throughout should be treated
as subject to quarterly review as new model generations are released.

More importantly: the governance criteria in this playbook - how to structure
human-agent handoffs, when to delegate vs. retain judgment, what constitutes a
well-scoped agent role - should also be reviewed at each major AI generation shift,
not just the tool landscape. When the next generation arrives, the right question
is not which tools changed but which governance assumptions changed.
