## The Problem

The current your role still matters framing is emotionally correct but analytically
insufficient. Readers who have seen displacement data will trust Tim more if he names
it directly and explains why this playbook guidance remains relevant.

---

## Recommended Sidebar

Title: The Honest Picture on Displacement Risk

Para 1 - Name the real signal:
The Anthropic Economic Index (March 2026) and Brynjolfsson et al. AI productivity
research show the same pattern: AI displacement is appearing first in entry cohort
hiring freezes among early-career professionals in high-exposure occupations.
This is not reassuring. It is a leading indicator.

Para 2 - Apply to the reader:
If you are a business-side product professional in a mid-to-large org, your direct
displacement risk is moderate-to-low in the near term. Your exposure is not from
being replaced outright - it is from your role scope narrowing, your headcount
ceiling dropping, and your promotion pathway becoming more contested.

Para 3 - Why this playbook still matters:
The practitioners best positioned in this environment built the judgment infrastructure
- the evaluation criteria, governance architecture, and human-AI handoff design -
that AI cannot replicate. That is what this playbook builds.
