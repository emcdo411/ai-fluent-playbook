## Module C: Snowflake Equation Lab v4 - Structural Force Audit

Three structural forces block implementation - not individual skill or willingness:
1. Speed Pressure Override: org-level velocity demands short-circuit HITL governance
2. Psychological Safety deficit: low-safety teams cannot safely experiment
3. Identity Threat from AI competence visibility: status risk inhibits public learning

All three are framed as individual mindset challenges. They should be named as
organizational structural conditions. Ch. 9 and Ch. 12 are the remedies.
See docs/v3-additions/structural-force-audit.md

Identity Crystallization Risk:
Practitioners in an identity-crystallized avoidance stance may read You are not late
as validation of avoidance rather than a call to action. A pattern disruption move
before the reassurance would reach the avoidant reader the current framing misses.
